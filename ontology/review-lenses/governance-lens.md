# Governance Lens

## Focus

Evaluate whether the event respects approval, reversibility, auditability, and human escalation boundaries.

## Use This Lens For

Use this lens when reviewing permission-sensitive actions, regulated workflows, or changes with irreversible consequences.

## Not This Lens For

Do not use this lens for low-risk autonomous behavior unless the action affects authority, traceability, or escalation.

## Questions

- What is the approval boundary, and what event or condition crosses it?
- Is the action reversible?
- Is the action auditable?
- Is permission sensitivity modeled correctly?
- What decision points or triggers require a human to be brought in?
