# Contrarian Lens

## Focus

Evaluate whether the claim is overstated, weakly evidenced, or built on a broken inference chain.

## Use This Lens For

Use this lens when reviewing bold claims, trend language, or mechanism narratives that need skepticism and evidence checking.

## Not This Lens For

Do not use this lens for routine implementation review unless the claim depends on fragile or speculative evidence.

## Questions

- Is marketing language being mistaken for mechanism?
- Is a small sample being inflated into a trend?
- Is a feature change being overstated as a paradigm change?
- Where is the evidence chain broken, and what specific missing evidence blocks the claim?
- What missing evidence would overturn the current claim?
