# Brand Lens

## Focus

Evaluate whether the event reinforces the product's identity, story, and category position.

## Use This Lens For

Use this lens when reviewing narrative shifts, positioning claims, and changes that affect how the market should describe the product.

## Not This Lens For

Do not use this lens for generic feature delivery unless the change alters the product's identity or category meaning.

## Questions

- If this change were removed, would the product's identity or category position materially weaken?
- Is narrative aligned with mechanism?
- What evidence shows this creates an ownable category distinction rather than feature-level noise?
