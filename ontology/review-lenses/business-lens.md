# Business Lens

## Focus

Evaluate whether the event is supported by business constraints, economics, and portfolio priority evidence.

## Use This Lens For

Use this lens when reviewing prioritization, revenue impact, cost structure, or organizational readiness.

## Not This Lens For

Do not use this lens for mechanism design or UX questions unless they materially change economics or priority.

## Questions

- What business evidence or constraints justify prioritization?
- What organizational preconditions are required?
- What evidence suggests the ROI can persist beyond launch, novelty, or initial adoption?
- Does it alter distribution or monetization logic?
