## Content

OPPO’s official framing is to make AI phones accessible and bring Google Gemini into core OPPO apps.
The emphasis is on practical AI features, broad availability, and upgraded everyday use cases.
Compared with Samsung or Xiaomi, OPPO’s story keeps the capability boundary closer to application enhancement than to a strong autonomous agent shell.
This places OPPO closer to an AI-feature system strategy, where the frontstage experience is improved but not fully rewritten as agent collaboration.
